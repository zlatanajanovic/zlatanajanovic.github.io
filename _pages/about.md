Hello,

I am a roboticist researching AI methods for machine learning and reasoning to enable autonomous robots in the wild.

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Chair of Machine Learning and Reasoning at RWTH Aachen University with Prof. Hector Geffner. Earlier, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Cognitive Robotics Department (CoR) at Delft University of Technology (Netherlands) with Prof. Jens Kober. My background includes a PhD in Automation and Control from Graz University of Technology and an MSc from the University of Sarajevo. As a Marie Sk≈Çodowska-Curie Fellow, I conducted research at VIRTUAL VEHICLE Research GmbH and held visiting roles at TU Delft, the University of Sarajevo, AVL List, and Volvo Cars.

For my work, I have received several best paper awards and the Hans List Award for my PhD thesis.
